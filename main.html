In 8085 Instruction set, SBI is a mnemonic that stands for “Subtract with Borrow Immediate from Accumulator” and here d8 stands for any 8-bit data as operand. This instruction is used to subtract 8-bit immediate data from the Accumulator along with the carry (borrow) value.
